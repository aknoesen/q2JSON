**MANDATORY: All mathematical expressions, variables, units, and symbols MUST be written using LaTeX syntax.**

**DO NOT USE Unicode characters under any circumstances. This includes, but is not limited to:**

- ❌ Ω → ✅ Use `$\Omega$`

- ❌ ² → ✅ Use `$^2$`
- ❌ μ → ✅ Use `$\mu$`

**✅ REQUIRED: Use LaTeX for all units and math:**
